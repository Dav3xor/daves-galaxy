About this Image

This is Dave's Galaxy, rendered as a 3000x3000 pixel png. each pixel represents one game unit in size, the stars are generally from .06 to .22 game units wide, and are slightly magnified when drawn to bring out more color.

The 2nd image is the galaxy, with color showing which players own which sectors within the game. I'll be making this clickable soon, but this will give you an idea of who's where, and you can hopefully find yourself.
