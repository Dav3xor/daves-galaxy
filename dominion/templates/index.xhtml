This Could be You!

Dave's Galaxy is a turn-based 'Beer and Pretzels' strategy game designed for casual play. Large amounts of time are not required, but daily usage is encouraged. We want to be a small but interesting part of your day, something you look forward to doing but are not consumed by.

Dave's Galaxy is loosely inspired by a popular BBS door game from the late 80's (Dominions), the game puts you in the position of the leader of a planet that has just discovered interstellar travel.

Procedurally Generated Game Universe

Demo

The game unfolds at a relaxed pace, spreading out over several months. It's perfectly ok to miss playing for several days in a row. Your empire will not fail if you go away on vacation for a week.

You can make colonies on neighboring planets, build them through trade and protect them from other players. As each planet increases in population and sophistication, you are able to build more and different types of ships there.

The game universe is very large; consisting of over 200,000 planets. There is plenty of room for expansion and new players.

Game play is centered around interacting with other players, and not necessarily by conquering them. There are many paths to greatness in Dave's Galaxy, only one of which is taking territory by force.
