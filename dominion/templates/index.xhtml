Welcome to New Dominion

the "10 minutes a day to conquer the galaxy" game.

New Dominion is a turn based strategy game designed for the casual gamer. Large amounts of time are not required to play, but daily usage is encouraged. We want to be a small but interesting part of your day, something you look forward to doing, but are not consumed by.

Game play is centered around interacting with other players, and not necessarily by conquering them. There are many paths to greatness in New Dominion, only one of which is taking territory by force.

New Dominion is loosely inspired by a popular BBS door game from the late 80's (Dominions!), the game puts you in the position of the leader of a planet that has just discovered interstellar travel.
